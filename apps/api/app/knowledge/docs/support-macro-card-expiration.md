# Support Macro - Card Expiration Reminder

Use this approved draft for failed renewals caused by expired cards or missing billing-owner notices.

## Draft

Hi {{billing_contact_name}}, your June renewal did not complete because the payment method on file appears to have expired. Please update the card from Billing Settings, then reply here so our team can retry the invoice.

## Notes

Do not blame the checkout retry workflow unless the invoice failure reason explicitly mentions retry suppression.
