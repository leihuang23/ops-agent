# Troubleshooting Enterprise Onboarding Rollout Risk

Enterprise rollout risk appears when sponsors cannot complete onboarding before procurement review. It is often visible in account tickets before cancelation.

## Signals

- Admins ask for executive summaries or implementation timelines.
- Account health score drops below 45.
- Subscription cancelation reason mentions onboarding or procurement.
- June invoice is void, not failed.
