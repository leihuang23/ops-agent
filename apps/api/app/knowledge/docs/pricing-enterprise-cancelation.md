# Enterprise Cancelation and Procurement Hold Notes

Enterprise accounts may cancel or pause renewal when rollout risk is unresolved before procurement review. These accounts usually show canceled subscriptions, void future invoices, and support tickets from sponsors or admins.

## Evidence Pattern

- Subscription status is canceled.
- Cancelation reason mentions onboarding, rollout, procurement, or sponsor risk.
- June invoice may be void rather than failed.
- Tickets often ask for an implementation summary or executive escalation.

## Recommended Response

Prepare win-back outreach and a concise onboarding blocker summary. Do not classify void invoices as payment failures.
