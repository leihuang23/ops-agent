# Support Macro - Billing Retry Follow-up

Use this draft only after approval when a customer reports a failed renewal despite updating the card.

## Draft

Hi {{billing_contact_name}}, we found that your renewal payment did not complete because our retry workflow did not submit the expected second charge attempt. We are correcting the retry and will confirm once the invoice is recovered.

## Required Evidence

Attach invoice ID, account ID, failure reason, and the support ticket ID. Never send this macro without approval from Revenue Operations.
