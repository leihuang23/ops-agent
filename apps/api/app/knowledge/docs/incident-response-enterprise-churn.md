# Incident Response - Enterprise Churn Wave

Enterprise churn waves usually combine account tickets, sponsor escalation, canceled subscriptions, and void invoices. They should not be mixed with active failed renewal recovery.

## Investigation

Check account health, cancelation reason, ticket sentiment, and onboarding blocker history. Procurement language in tickets is a strong indicator that the root cause is rollout risk rather than payment failure.

## Recommended Actions

Prepare executive win-back notes and summarize unresolved onboarding blockers by account.
