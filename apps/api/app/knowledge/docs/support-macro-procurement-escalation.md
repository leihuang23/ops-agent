# Support Macro - Procurement Escalation

Use this draft for enterprise sponsors who paused renewal because onboarding risk remains unresolved.

## Draft

Hi {{sponsor_name}}, we reviewed the rollout blockers attached to your procurement review. We are preparing a focused recovery plan that lists owner, status, and next milestone for each blocker.

## Notes

This macro is not appropriate for failed payment or card expiration cases. Cite the cancelation reason and account escalation ticket.
